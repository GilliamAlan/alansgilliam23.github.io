Big snowstorm forecast to dump up to a foot of snow on East Idaho starting Tuesday

Article by the Idaho State Journal Staff

Eastern Idahoans will likely have more snow to shovel on Tuesday and Wednesday as another big winter storm barrels through the region.

This new storm comes on the heels of the multi-day storm that delivered a snowy Ocotber and blizzard conditions to Idaho.

The National Weather Service confirmed that this Friday to Halloween storm was the most powerful of the season thus far to hit East Idaho, dumping over 2 feet of snow on some areas.
