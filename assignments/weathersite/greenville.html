Greenville Weather News

Sunny Summer Activities

The city of Greenville encourges all residents and visitors to stay safe this summer as they enjoy the many activities at the lake. Remember sunscreen, life vests, and the buddy system for fun in the sun.

Spring Runoff Report

River flows are high and fast due to an exceptionally high spring runoff. Please exercise extra caution near waterways. If your property is in danger of flooding visit city hall to request sandbags.

School District Announcements

Graduation is May 29th and the last day of school for all other district students is May 30th. School will begin again August 18th. Enjoy your summer vacation!
